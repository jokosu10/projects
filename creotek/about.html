About CreoTek Solutions

CreoTek Solutions is a software development company specializing in software design, development, consultation and managed-services focusing on mobile, cloud, systems and emerging technologies such as Virtual and Augmented Reality.

We are committed in delighting the customers by delivering innovative products, technical support, and after-sales services to meet the ever-changing demands of our customers. All these things contribute to our commitment to superior customer experience believing successful software projects requires comprehensive design, strong technical skills, combined with creativity insight.
